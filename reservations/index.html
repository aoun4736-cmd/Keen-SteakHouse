The Legendary
Mutton Chop

Our signature Mutton Chop is not goat—it is a saddle of lamb, aged to perfection, a cut unique to Keens since 1885. Each 26-ounce masterpiece is hand-cut and broiled to your specification, served with our house-made mint jelly.

This is not simply a meal. It is a rite of passage for those who appreciate the art of meat at its finest.

Dress Code

Keens maintains a tradition of elegant dining. We kindly request Business Casual attire. Jackets are appreciated but not required for gentlemen.

Athletic wear, shorts, and flip-flops are not permitted in the dining rooms. We reserve the right to provide a jacket if needed.
